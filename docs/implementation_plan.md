# Implementation Plan for TRY-ON App

This document summarizes the major features required for the TRY-ON Shopify app as discussed in the project requirements.

## 1. Domain and Access
- Connect `tryonyou.app` in Shopify and disable the storefront password.

## 2. Product Catalog
- Upload at least 20 real products with attractive names, SEO-friendly descriptions and high-quality images.
- Ensure pricing, stock levels, categories and Pau size system (height + width 1‑2‑3) are configured.

## 3. Pau – The Virtual Stylist
- Add a "Meet Pau" section with a short, friendly backstory and an animation or illustration.
- Integrate Pau into the virtual fitting room so users can interact with it.

## 4. Virtual Fitting Room
- Embed or link the fitting room from the home page and product pages.
- Provide instructions for measuring and deliver personalized size recommendations.

## 5. Home Page
- Include a striking hero image or video with a call to action.
- Summarize the concept (fashion, Pau and fitting room) in three lines.
- Add active buttons: **Shop Now**, **Discover Pau**, and **Try the Fitting Room**.

## 6. Legal Pages
- Prepare terms and conditions, privacy policy, shipping and returns policy, and a contact page.

## 7. Language Support
- Offer the site in English (optionally French and Spanish) with a consistent brand voice.

## 8. Orders and Automation
- Connect Shopify with Make and Firebase for stock and order automation.
- Activate and test Shopify Payments or Stripe.

## 9. Mobile and Navigation
- Ensure a fully responsive layout with a clear, fast navigation menu.

## 10. Emotion and Branding
- Use visuals or text that inspire excitement and talk about “your best self,” “fun,” and “unique design.”
- Include at least one memorable phrase signed by Pau.

---

This checklist can be used to track progress as the project evolves.
